  • 2018

    Our Beginnings

    Cotacachi Special Olympics was initiated approximately four years ago. This Club was started because there was recognition that students with Special Needs, that did not attend special schools, had limited to no social outlets, including the opportunities to practice and compete in events.

  • 2018

    Motivation

    Initially, a retired special educator found herself working with two children with Downs Syndrome. Through discussions with the families, they identified that there were many families in town where special services were nonexistent. These discussions were the impetus of the club as it is today.

  • First Steps

    We began an outreach to families in our area. We had several meetings, and ultimately had 20 athletes of varying ages. Practices began, but then we met the next challenge. Since Special Olympics teams were affiliated with Special Schools, ongoing competitions at the Provincial level were not held. The one school in Ibarra that had begun a team, were the Provincial Team. Competitions with other teams were not held because there were no other teams to compete with. The Cotacachi Club collaborated with the existing Provincial Team to begin competitions, allowing several of our athletes to go on and compete in National events.

  • 2020-2022

    The Pandemic

    The Pandemic slowed everything and everyone down. We did not practice nor compete for two years to assure the safety of our athletes. At this point in time, we have once again started to practice and prepare for upcoming events. We hope for games in May 2022, as well as a soccer competition with the Police department in June 2022.
